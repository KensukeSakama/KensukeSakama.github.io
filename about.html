About

I am a software engineer with a background in project management and sales in the automotive industry. Though I still hold my love for cars, I found myself being more interested in the thought process in engineering as I worked in teams with so many brilliant engineers. Back in early 2019, I decided to pick up coding. I have been enjoying it since!

As a software engineer, I love the process of turning an idea into a product and solving problems along the way. I enjoy learning about new tools and technologies to discover why and how they were developed, as it often gives me a new perspective.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy rock climbing. Ironic enough, I find it oddly similar to how I approach coding. I break down a problem into smaller sections to reach the end goal. One section may be more challenging than the others, but I thrive on training and finding a solution one piece at a time and put them together to get to the top. It is a great feeling to look back and see what I have learned from the process.

I currently live in San Diego, CA with a large tortoise shell cat.
